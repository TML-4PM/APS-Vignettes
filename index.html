Introduction: Explore the Future of APS with agenticAI. It's ok!

Welcome to a collection of case studies exploring the future of the Australian Public Service (APS) through the lens of "Augmented Humanity," brought to you by agenticAI. It's ok!. Set in the year 2026, these narratives delve into hypothetical scenarios where technology and human ingenuity converge to create a more efficient, responsive, and citizen-centric public service.

"Augmented Humanity" in this context refers to the strategic integration of advanced technologies, including artificial intelligence, to enhance the capabilities of APS staff, streamline processes, and ultimately deliver superior outcomes for the Australian people. It's not about replacing human judgment, but empowering it; not about diminishing the human touch, but extending its reach and impact.

This site offers 100 illustrative case studies. You can browse all case studies by category, gain strategic insights from our Summary page, find answers in the FAQ, explore visionary scenarios on our Future Ready page, or read Ministerial Perspectives. Each story aims to spark discussion and inspire innovative thinking.
